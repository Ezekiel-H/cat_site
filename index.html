Services

The Cats United Neighbourhood Trust on Hunstbury is a defacto shelter for all neighbourhood cats. Simply have your cat come onto our Neighbourhood sanctuary and they will be welcomed into their new home.

Feline Freinds

Sick of your cat? Does it eat too much? Does it scratch too much? Does it piss in Poppa's wine cellar? Let us take it off your hands - no questions asked.

Accomodation

We pride ourselves on making an inclusive envioroment for all cats. Not only does the sanctuary offer stunning views of Christchurch City, it also features as retro style and ever changing garden landscape.

Cat Nurse On Site

We proudly have a registered cat nurse residing in house. Her 34 year expeerince ranges from new mothers (with her offspring), pirate cats right through to getting homeless cats off the street.
